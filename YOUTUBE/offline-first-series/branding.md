# Branding Notes

## Channel Vibe

- **Tone:** Confident, anti-subscription, not preachy
- **Energy:** Libertarian undertones, "fuck you" to rent-seeking
- **Audience:** Artists, indie devs, tool builders
- **Promise:** "Your data. Your models. Your art. Your studio."

## Visual Style

- **Color palette:** Dark mode, high contrast, neon accents
- **Font:** Clean monospace for code, bold sans for titles
- **Editing:** Fast cuts, snappy pacing, no dead air

## Thumbnail Ideas

### Episode 1: "Your Computer Isn't a Dumb Terminal"
- Laptop with ethernet cable plugged in
- "OFFLINE MODE" badge prominently displayed
- Subtle middle finger energy in composition
- Text: "YOUR DATA. YOUR COMPUTER."

## Tagline Options

- "Your apps don't need the internet. They need you to give a shit."
- "Stop renting. Start owning."
- "The cloud is just someone else's computer that's worse than yours."
- "5% utilization. 100% subscription. That ends today."
